Welcome to Customer Satisfaction Predictor

In today's fast-paced business world, understanding customer satisfaction is more important than ever. This platform uses the power of Machine Learning (ML), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and MLOps (Machine Learning Operations) to analyze customer feedback in real-time.

Our system processes unstructured data such as customer reviews and comments to determine the sentiment — whether it's Positive, Neutral, or Negative. This helps companies make smarter, data-driven decisions to improve their services and customer experiences.
